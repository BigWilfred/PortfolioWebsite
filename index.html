Hi :) I am Glenn, a designer from Brisbane Queensland. My recently completed undergraduate degree has provided me with the theoretical and technological processes of creating meaningful design. I am now excited to grow these skill in a practical manner and welcome the opportunity to become a valuable member of a team.

My design approach is user-centric and driven by test results. Through experience, I understand that I am not the end-used, welcoming critique and am open to change. I love coming up with new ideas and am confident in putting forward suggestions within a team environment.
