About

I am Glenn, a designer from Brisbane Queensland. I graduated from the University of Queensland at the end of 2018, completing my undergraduate Multimedia Design degree. I am excited to grow my existing skills through practical experience, understanding the theoretical and technological components that create usable projects.

My design approach is user-centric and driven by testing input. Through experience, I understand that I am not the end-used, welcoming critique and open to change. I love coming up with new ideas and am confident to put forward suggestions within a team environment.
